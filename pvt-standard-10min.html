Instructions

Your Task: Monitor a red box on the screen. When yellow numbers appear, press the spacebar as quickly as possible.

Important Guidelines:

  • React quickly: Press spacebar immediately when you see the yellow counter appear
  • Don't anticipate: Don't press too early; you will get an error if you press before the numbers appear
  • Stay focused: The test lasts 10 minutes. Keep your attention on the red box throughout
  • Accurate performance: Your reaction time will be measured in milliseconds

What You'll See:

  • A red monitoring box in the center of the screen
  • Yellow numbers will appear at random intervals (approximately 2-10 seconds apart)
  • You will receive feedback after each response (approximately 1 second)
  • The numbers will indicate your reaction time in milliseconds

Scoring:

  • Correct (green): Reaction time between 100-500 ms
  • Too Slow (red): Reaction time > 500 ms (lapse)
  • Error (red): Pressing too early (< 100 ms) or before stimulus appears
